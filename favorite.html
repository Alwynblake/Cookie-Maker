Oatmeal Cinnamon Coconut Cookies

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1 cup sifted flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup uncooked quick oats
  • 1 cup coconut
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

    Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugar together
  2. 'Beat in eggs, milk and vanilla'
  3. 'Add and mix in dry ingredients except oats'
  4. When smooth, mix in oats
  5. Mix in Coconut
  6. Mix in cinnamon
  7. Beat mixture well
  8. Drop cookies in 1 inch balls onto a greased cookie sheet
  9. Bake 10 to 12 minutes. Cool on wire wrack

Double Chocolate Chip with Nuts

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1 teaspon Vanilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup chopped nuts

    Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 375
  2. Cream butter and sugar together
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  4. Mix in Cocoa Powder
  5. Beat in flour, salt and baking powder
  6. Mix in chocolate chips
  7. Mix in Nuts
  8. Drop balls into one inch balls into an ungreased cookie tray
  9. Bake 9 to 11 minutes, cool on a wire rack
